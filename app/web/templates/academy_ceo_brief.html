CEO Brief: PPC + product timing

The goal is simple: spot demand shifts early, then move budgets and launches before competitors react.

What changes
  • Faster keyword discovery (new rising terms)
  • Less reactive PPC (shift spend earlier)
  • Better product timing (launch before peak)
KPIs to watch
  • CTR / CVR on new rising terms
  • Acos / Tacos trend for “early” groups
  • Share of spend moved to rising terms
Rollout (7 days)
  1. Pick 1 category + 1 hero ASIN
  2. Run weekly scan (same range each week)
  3. Promote top 10 terms into a test campaign
  4. Scale winners, kill losers fast

How to use it in PPC (simple rule)

If a term is consistently improving (rank getting better) across your selected weeks, treat it as an “early signal” keyword. Start small (exact + phrase), measure, then scale.
